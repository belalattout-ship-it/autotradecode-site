FAQ

Do you provide a ready-made “winning strategy”?

No. We only code your rules. If you don’t have a clear idea yet, we can help you structure it, but we don’t sell signals or secret strategies.

Can you connect to my live account?

The bot runs using your API keys on your own machine or VPS. We don’t ask for your login, only the API permissions needed for trading according to your exchange’s rules.

How long does it take?

Simple bots: ~24–48 hours. More complex builds or anything with unusual logic: we’ll give you a realistic estimate after reading your brief.
