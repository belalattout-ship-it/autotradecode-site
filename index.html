Process

How a typical project runs

Simple steps, clear communication. You always know what I’m building and why.

01 · Strategy brief
You describe the rules

You send your idea in plain English: instruments, timeframes, entry/exit logic, risk per trade and any filters. Screenshots and TradingView links are welcome.

02 · Plan & scope
We pick the right package

I confirm what’s feasible, clarify edge cases and suggest the right package (Basic, Pro, Premium), plus any add-ons like indicators or VPS setup if needed.

03 · Build & verify
I code and test the logic

Your rules are implemented and tested on sample data or demo conditions. I check signals and behavior match what you described, and adjust if anything is off.

04 · Delivery & support
You receive code & guidance

You get full source code, basic documentation and setup help. If the bot misbehaves relative to the agreed rules, I fix it — not bill you again.
