How it works

From idea to finished bot

Four clear steps. You always know what I’m building and why.

Step 1 · Strategy brief

You describe your rules in plain English: markets, timeframe, entry, exit and risk. Screenshots and example trades are welcome.

Step 2 · Scope & quote

I confirm what’s feasible, highlight any unclear cases and suggest the right package from the Pricing page.

Step 3 · Build & test

I implement your rules and test them in demo conditions. You can compare behaviour with your manual trading.

Step 4 · Delivery & support

You receive the code, basic documentation and setup help. If the bot doesn’t match the agreed rules, I fix it.

FAQ

Common questions

If you still have questions after this, just send them in the contact form.

Do you create strategies or give financial advice?

No. I don’t design strategies or tell you what to trade. I take your existing rules and express them as code.

Can you guarantee profit?

No performance is guaranteed. A bot is just a tool that follows your rules more consistently and faster than a human.

What do you need from me to start?

Platform (for example TradingView, MT4, Binance), instruments, timeframe, entry and exit rules, and basic risk logic.

Will I get the source code?

Yes. You always receive the full source code for the agreed platform, not an encrypted or locked file.
